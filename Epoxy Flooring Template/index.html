Our Method

An Obsession
With Preparation

The difference between a floor that fails in three years and one that endures for thirty is invisible to the naked eye. It lives in the substrate. Every Aura installation begins long before a drop of resin is poured.

01

Moisture Assessment

Calibrated moisture vapor emission testing is performed before a single tool touches the substrate. Resin and water are incompatible — we eliminate the variable entirely.

02

Diamond Grinding & Profiling

Heavy-duty planetary grinders achieve a precise Concrete Surface Profile (CSP 3–4), opening the pores and creating mechanical adhesion that no primer alone can replicate.

03

Precision Resin Application

A multi-layer system — primer, body coat, broadcast, and seal — applied with zero margin for error. Timing, temperature, and technique are monitored at every stage.

04

Urethane Topcoat Sealing

A UV-stable, chemical-resistant polyurea or polyaspartic armor coat completes the system — engineered for decade-long performance under real-world conditions.

No shortcuts. No compromises. Ever.
